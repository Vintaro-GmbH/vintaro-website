3. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to information (Art. 15 GDPR)
  • Right to correction (Art. 16 GDPR)
  • Right to deletion (Art. 17 GDPR)
  • Right to restriction of processing (Art. 18 GDPR)
  • Right to data portability (Art. 20 GDPR)
  • Right to object (Art. 21 GDPR)

4. Data Collection

When you visit our website, our web server automatically collects the following technical data:

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system used
  • Referrer URL
  • Date and time of access
  • IP address

This data is stored in log files for a maximum of 7 days and is then automatically deleted. The legal basis for this data processing is Art. 6 para. 1 lit. f GDPR (legitimate interests).


5. Cookies

This website currently does not use cookies.


6. Google Fonts

This website uses Google Fonts for the uniform display of fonts. When you access a page, your browser loads the required fonts into your browser cache to display texts and fonts correctly. For this purpose, the browser you are using must connect to Google's servers. This gives Google knowledge that our website has been accessed via your IP address. The use of Google Fonts is in the interest of a uniform and appealing presentation of our online offers. This represents a legitimate interest within the meaning of Art. 6 para. 1 lit. f GDPR.
